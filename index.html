Best Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

This hearty and soothing chicken noodle soup is a timeless comfort food, perfect for chilly days or when you're under the weather. Made with tender chicken, wholesome vegetables, and egg noodles in a savory broth, it’s simple, nourishing, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken (use rotisserie or poached chicken)
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Juice of ½ lemon (optional, for brightness)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5–6 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  2. Pour in chicken broth. Stir in thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  4. Add egg noodles and simmer for 8–10 minutes, or until noodles are al dente. Stir in shredded chicken and simmer for another 2–3 minutes to heat through.
  5. Discard bay leaf. Stir in parsley and lemon juice if using. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve hot.
